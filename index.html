MTG Proxy Printing Tool

(1) Find and select cards you want from EDHREC.
(2) Export your selection as `CLIPBOARD`. 
(3) Paste it to the text area, and click `Generate Proxies`. 
Eg:
Armasaur Guide
Dazzling Angel
Exemplar of Light
Hinterland Sanctifier
Leyline of Hope
Qala, Ajani's Pridemate
